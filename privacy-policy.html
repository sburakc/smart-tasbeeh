Privacy Policy

Smart Tasbeeh — Last updated: February 24, 2026

Smart Tasbeeh does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully respected.

Data Collection

Smart Tasbeeh does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or registration required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or ad networks
  • No cookies or device identifiers
  • No data sent to any server

Local Data Storage

All your data (counting history, settings, custom dhikr) is stored locally on your device only using an offline database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Permissions

Smart Tasbeeh may request the following device permissions:

  • Vibration/Haptics — For tactile feedback when counting. No data is collected through this feature.

Third-Party Services

Smart Tasbeeh does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data. There are no ads, no analytics platforms, and no social media integrations.

Children's Privacy

Smart Tasbeeh is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any data whatsoever, there are no concerns regarding children's privacy (COPPA compliance).

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Since the app collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.
